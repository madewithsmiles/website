Projects with Intelligent Software

We are a group of UC Berkeley students that solve problems by building intelligent software. By connecting research in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science, our teams build projects that impact people globally.

Research, Development, Interdisciplinary

By promoting research in frontier areas of computer science and working with modern techniques, we build projects with a purpose that impact communities and people globally.

Artificial Intelligence

We build modern applications that can adapt and improved in response to user behavior and human interaction.

Machine Learning

Through neural networks and data analytics, the performance of our projects are accurate and reliable.

Data Science

Our projects are data driven by large datasets so that we can build scalable and intuitive software.
