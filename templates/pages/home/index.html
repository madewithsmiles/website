Projects with Intelligent Software

We are a group of UC Berkeley students that solve problems by building intelligent software. By connecting research in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science, our teams build projects that impact people globally.

Research, Development, Interdisciplinary

By connecting teams to research in computer science, developing with modern techniques and technology, and targeting specific disciplines, we build projects with a purpose - that impact communities and people globally.

Artificial Intelligence

We build modern applications that can adapt and react based user behavior and human interaction.

Machine Learning

Through neural networks and data analytics, the performance of our projects are accurate and reliable.

Data Science

Our projects are data driven by large datasets so that we can build scalable and intuitive software.

Traditions

In our family, we believe in working as a team to build projects, host events, and find opportunities in the industry.

Workflow

Since we value a personal and inclusive environment, we do not expect our members to work alone or outside of organized meetings.

Tech Tutorials

Our members attend tutorials on the software and theory that they need to build the project and work together.

Hack Nights

Most of our software is built at hack nights where the project leader and developers hack in an intense 4-5 hour session.

Events

Throughout the semester, we host events for our members and the CS community at UC Berkeley.

"Battle of the Nerds" Hackathon

We invite clubs on campus to send teams of 5 to represent their club and battle in a competition of speed, wit, and innovation.

Recruiting Workshops

We offer exclusive recruiting workshops for our members to find opportunities in the industry including resumes and networking.

The Future of Machines and Software

Every semester, we invite leaders in the industry to talk about the latest trends in artificial intelligence and virtual reality.
